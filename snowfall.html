Snowfall Record Cover

This was another Uni Project where we had to analyse a piece of music and create an accompanying record cover for it. I had chosen the song "Snowfall" by Øneheart and had decided to create an accompanying 3D world around the feelings that listening to the song had evoked. It was something that felt tranquil and peaceful and something that is found in isolation so I had created a sort of camping site as a way to distance away from everyone and evoke that isolated feeling that I had felt.
