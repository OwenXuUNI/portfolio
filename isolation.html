Isolation

Isolation was a short film I made entirely using the 3d software Blender. It was a part of a submission for the Matthew Koefoed Scholarship in NZ and is still a work I am pretty proud of. The film itself follows a man who is grows tired of the same mundane life where he is just stuck by himself, with no one to lean on his mind wanders to look for an escape through all this but even through everything it all revolves back to square one. It aimed to highlight how difficult it is for people to escape this sort of machine that we have in society and aims to make us question it.
